Welcome to the Community Library

a fullstack project by LaRena Iocco.
This idea was inspired by a variety of things:
  • My love of books and the library
  • My inability to go to the library due to the pandemic
  • The media exchange table at my local park
  • "Buy Nothing" groups on social media
What if you could be a part of an online community that was centered around your geographic location and find books to borrow from your neighbors?
Feel free to explore the search functionality and sample books without signing up for an account!
